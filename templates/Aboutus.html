About Us


We are a team of students from the Research Center for Genetics and Reproductive Health (CGRH), University of Health Sciences - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City. Motivated by our dedication to scientific research and healthcare, we developed this website to support clinicians in the early non-invasive diagnosis of CRC through gene expression-based prediction.
